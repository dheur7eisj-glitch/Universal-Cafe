Our Story

Born from a Love
of Great Coffee

Universal Cafe was founded with a simple dream — to create a space where every person who walks in feels instantly at home. Nestled in the vibrant neighbourhood of Jharoda Kalan, Delhi, just a stone's throw from Evergreen Public School, we've grown into the community's most beloved hangout.

We source our coffee beans from the finest estates across Coorg and Chikmagalur, roasted to perfection so every cup carries warmth, character, and depth. Our kitchen crafts fresh snacks and bakes daily — because we believe great food deserves great coffee.
